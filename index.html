About

I’m a senior cameraman with extensive experience operating high‑definition camera systems for major broadcast productions. With a background in electrical engineering and project management, I approach every shoot with a technical, problem‑solving mindset that keeps workflows smooth and setups reliable. What drives me is the power of visual storytelling. I’m passionate about using light, composition, and motion to create images that resonate. Through this blog, I share the techniques, insights, and behind‑the‑lens moments that shape my work.
